Real Name: Kim Namjoon (김남준)
Birthday: September 12, 1994
Zodiac sign: Virgo
Height: 181 cm (5’11”)
Weight: 73.6 kg (162 lbs)
Blood Type: A
MBTI Type: ENFP
Twitter account (all BTS members share 1 single twitter account): @bts_twt

RM facts:
